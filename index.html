Plagued

"Plagued" is my final project for the RC drawing 288 class. My drawings are themed around the Flint water crisis. I drew inspiration from the Disney princess characters and added some twist to them, creating 9 pieces of drawings to satirize the government and the whole incident. The final product is an artist book with both the illustrations and poems. The design of the artist book is simple and elegant. I like the comparison between how complex the drawings are and how plain the book cover is.

  • Client: Start Bootstrap
  • Date: April 2014
  • Service: Web Development
